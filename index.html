About Us

Hi! We're WEON STUDIOS.

A small but passionate team of pixel-art lovers, world-builders, and game dreamers.

Pixel by pixel, we create experiences that blend style and meaning - from interactive stories to immersive spaces.

Whether it's designing gameplay or crafting architectural visualizations, we're here to make ideas come alive.

What we do

We work across two main areas:

Game development - where creativity runs wild, gameplay matters, and every detail counts.

ArchViz - where we help bring imagined environments into vivid, pixel-perfect form.

In everything we do, we stick to three things:
Precision - Expression - Care.

Because for us, it's not just about making something that looks good. It's about making something that feels right.

Passion in each pixel.
That's our way of working, and our way of thinking.

Our Philosophy

At our core, we believe ideas are meant to be shared.

Our studio isn't just a dev team - it's a space where creativity becomes real, pixels tells stories, and every project is a new chance to explore, learn and connect.

We aim for
quality over quantity

And while we're just getting started, we're here to make it count — one pixel at a time.
